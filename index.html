Zero-Energy Houses

Our principle aims to design, model and realize an ecological, sustainable, productive and innovative habitat with zero energy consumption and inspired by traditional Moroccan architecture. This initiative is perfectly in line with the Moroccan national energy strategy in the property and planning sector.

HOW IT WORKS

TADD-ART is an ecological habitat that uses renewable energies as the sole source of energy and the Internet of Things to facilitate and automate the furniture use experience as well as other sustainable techniques based on bioclimatic design, citing the good orientation, ventilation and vegetation.

OUR GOALS

Build a home that is friendly, interactive, sustainable, intelligent, transformable and adaptable to the needs of multigenerational inhabitants, while preserving the Moroccan identity from design to reception.

Our passion

Techniques based on bioclimatic architecture. Solar systems, high technology opportunities including intelligent control, automation and measurement systems. Smart design and innovative business models.

ABOUT US

TDD-ART is the brainchild of the MU-ATA team formed by a collaboration between students and professors from MUNDIAPOLIS University of Casablanca and the Academy of Traditional Arts. The team mixes the motivations, enthusiasm and agility of new generations of engineering students with the experiences, skills, rigor and dynamism of architecture and engineering experts to promote a comprehensive goal.


WHO WE ARE ?

THE MU-ATA A TEAM has been selected from 20 multinational teams to participate in the solar decathlon Africa contest to bengirir in September 2019 the contest is to design a home to zero energy consumption our participation will be through our project home Tadd-art.
