Mundiapolis University & The Academy of Traditional Art

Mundiapolis University

Mundiapolis University of Casablanca is a private university, created in 1996 in Casablanca it began in 1996 with the creation of Polyfinance, the first specialized school in financial engineering in Morocco. in 1999: Creation of the Institute of Management and Company Law (IMADE), the first institute specializing in business law in Morocco. in 2002: Creation of the Moroccan School of Computer Science, Electronics and Automation (EMIAE). in 2009: Merging of the 3 schools to give birth to Mundiapolis Casablanca University, the first multidisciplinary private campus in Morocco. in 2012: Official recognition of the State Private University Label. Mundiapolis University has 6 teaching divisions: Faculty of Health Sciences, School of Engineering, Business School, Institute of Political, Legal and Social Sciences, Center of Preparatory Classes, Executive Training Center

The Academy of Traditional Art

Created October 31, 2012 under the high instructions of His Majesty King Mohammed VI, may God assist, the Academy of Traditional Arts is one of the components of the Foundation Hassan II Mosque Casablanca, which is a flat a form of excellence dedicated to high-level training in Traditional Arts, based on the acquisition of scientific knowledge and the development of virtuosities. This orientation has made the Academy of Traditional Arts a unique institution of its kind at the national level, rarely matched internationally and has given it the following characteristics: Academic space fulfilling the artistic and scientific conditions that allow master craftsmen to pass on to the new generations, with fidelity and probity, the trades and skills they inherited from their ancestors. It is also a place where the manual dexterity of trades and scientific, technological and historical knowledge coexist. A center of scientific research in the field of traditional arts through the exploration and archiving of the artistic specificities of traditional art and its preservation against any alteration. Establishment training new generations of craftsmen with the qualities required to innovate in the field without undermining the characteristic features of deep Moroccan craftsmanship. Reference institution in the field of research and expertise and a driving force for communication with sectors active in the traditional arts, in terms of production, authorization, research and promotion of products. Through the training that is provided, the Academy of Traditional Arts is called to contribute to the enrichment and enhancement of the national crafts sector, which will make it a vital link in the network of national actors international organizations concerned with traditional arts and related activities. Its role will cross the boundaries of economics to expand to the cultural, knowing that it will contribute to safeguarding, preserving and promoting the artistic and cultural aspects of our Moroccan identity.

ABOUT US

TDD-ART is the brainchild of the MU-ATA team formed by a collaboration between students and professors from MUNDIAPOLIS University of Casablanca and the Academy of Traditional Arts. The team mixes the motivations, enthusiasm and agility of new generations of engineering students with the experiences, skills, rigor and dynamism of architecture and engineering experts to promote a comprehensive goal.
